\section{Executive Preamble \& Recitals}
This **Letter of Intent** (the ``Letter'' or ``LOI'') is entered into as of May 22, 2026 (the ``Effective Date''), by and between:
\par\vspace{5pt}
\noindent
\textbf{Hyperion Realty (Dubai VASP)}, a virtual asset service provider under incorporation in the Emirate of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, representing its designated Single-Asset Special Purpose Vehicles (collectively, the \textbf{``Buyer''} or \textbf{``Hyperion''}), and
\par\vspace{5pt}
\noindent
\textbf{ASAP Holdings / ASAP International Hotel LLC}, a commercial real estate investment company and portfolio owner (collectively, the \textbf{``Seller''} or \textbf{``ASAP''}).

\subsection{Background and Intent}
Seller represents that it is the fee-simple owner or lawful controller of a premier commercial hospitality portfolio comprising ten (10) distinct assets, totalizing 1,852 keys, with a combined institutional fair market valuation of \textbf{\$815,000,000 USD} (the ``ASAP Portfolio''). 

Buyer wishes to acquire, and Seller wishes to sell, one hundred percent (100\%) of the legal ownership, title, and operating rights of the ASAP Portfolio. This transaction shall be executed through a highly structured, phased real estate tokenization program over a **six (6) to twelve (12) month period**, utilizing Hyperion's proprietary **HYPERION-20** compliant smart contract framework under licensing from the Dubai Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA).

% --- Section 4: Transaction Structure ---
\section{Tokenization \& Corporate Transaction Structure}
For each individual asset in the ASAP Portfolio, the transaction shall be executed according to the following compliant legal and cryptographic structure:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Single-Asset SPV}: Buyer shall establish a dedicated, single-asset Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) incorporated in either the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) or the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM), which SPV shall hold 100\% fee-simple title to the corresponding physical real estate.
    \item \textbf{HYPERION-20 Compliance}: Each SPV shall issue a unique, fractionalized smart contract token (complying with the HYPERION-20 standard). The tokens will natively enforce CCO transfer whitelisting, a 1.5\% secondary volume fee, and centralized liquidation freezes.
    \item \textbf{Purchase Consideration}: The Purchase Price for each asset shall be paid to Seller in:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item US Dollar (USD) or USD Coin (USDC) stablecoins derived from the public and institutional capital raises.
        \item Roll-over equity options, allowing Seller to hold up to twenty percent (20\%) of the issued HYPERION-20 SPV tokens, thereby retaining a fractional, yield-bearing interest in the properties.
    \end{itemize}
\end{enumerate}

% --- Section 5: Legal Protections ---
\section{Fiduciary Protections \& Transaction Contingencies}
To protect Hyperion Realty from capital market volatility, construction delays, or asset defects, the definitive agreements shall incorporate the following ironclad Fortune-500 legal protections:

\subsection{Tokenization Funding Contingency (The ``Shield'' Clause)}
The Buyer’s obligation to close the acquisition of any asset within the ASAP Portfolio is strictly and absolutely contingent upon the successful completion of the corresponding public or institutional capital offering of the respective HYPERION-20 token. 
\par\vspace{5pt}
If the Buyer fails to achieve the minimum required capital target for an asset by the end of its designated phase timeline:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Buyer shall have the unilateral right to terminate the transaction with respect to that specific asset without penalty, default, or liability of any kind.
    \item Buyer may, at its sole discretion, extend the phase timeline for up to an additional ninety (90) days by written notice to Seller.
    \item Seller shall have no recourse, claim, or cause of action against Hyperion or its affiliates for unsuccessful token offerings.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Rigorous Due Diligence Period}
Seller shall provide Buyer and its legal, technical, and financial advisors with complete, unrestricted access to the books, historical financial statements, tax records, engineering reports, environmental assessments, and franchise agreements of all ten (10) properties. 
\par\vspace{5pt}
Buyer shall have a period of **sixty (60) days** from the receipt of all requested disclosures (the ``Diligence Period'') to review the assets. If Buyer, in its sole and absolute discretion, is unsatisfied with the results of its due diligence, Buyer may terminate this LOI and the transaction in its entirety with immediate effect, and without further obligation or liability.

\subsection{Absolute Exclusivity and ``No-Shop'' Commitment}
In consideration of the significant expenses, legal structuring costs, and technical deployment assets Hyperion is committing to this transaction, Seller hereby covenants and agrees that for a period of **ninety (90) days** following the Effective Date (the ``Exclusivity Period''):
\begin{itemize}
    \item Seller, its officers, directors, shareholders, affiliates, and advisors shall not solicit, initiate, encourage, or entertain any offers, inquiries, or proposals from, nor engage in any discussions or negotiations with, any third party regarding the sale, transfer, lease, mortgage, or alternative tokenization of any asset within the ASAP Portfolio.
    \item Seller shall immediately notify Buyer of any unsolicited inquiries or alternative proposals received during this period.
    \item A breach of this Exclusivity Clause shall result in immediate injunctive relief and the Seller shall indemnify Buyer for all out-of-pocket legal, technical, and compliance expenses incurred.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Centralized Fiduciary Asset Management}
Following the acquisition of any asset, Hyperion shall act as the sole and centralized fiduciary asset manager of the property's holding SPV. The Seller and future token holders acknowledge and agree that token ownership does not confer voting rights, direct governance authority, or property management decisions. In the event of a portfolio liquidation or asset exit, Hyperion retains the unilateral fiduciary authority to execute the sale, distribute pro-rata USDC stablecoin redemptions via the HYPERION-20 smart contract, and freeze the tokens in perpetuity as digital mementos of ownership.

\section{Miscellaneous \& Binding Nature}

This Letter, and all disputes or claims arising out of or in connection with its subject matter, shall be governed by, and construed in accordance with, the laws of the **Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC)**, Emirate of Dubai, United Arab Emirates.
\par\vspace{5pt}
Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this LOI, including any question regarding its existence, validity, or termination, shall be referred to and finally resolved by binding arbitration under the Arbitration Rules of the **DIFC-LCIA Arbitration Centre** in force at the time. The seat of the arbitration shall be the DIFC, and the language of the proceedings shall be English.
