\section{Executive Statutory Framework}
Hyperion Realty operates as a centralized Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) under direct licensing from the **Dubai Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA)**. To guarantee absolute compliance with the laws of the Emirate of Dubai and the federal statutes of the United Arab Emirates, this internal document serves as our master cross-walk. It maps every operational policy, smart contract mechanic, and corporate charter to the exact regulatory standards, guidelines, and rulebooks enforced by VARA.

This mapping verifies that our centralized asset management framework, our custom **HYPERION-20** token standard, and our asset-level SPV isolations fulfill the highest institutional standard of fiduciary responsibility and consumer protection.
